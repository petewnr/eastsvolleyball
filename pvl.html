pvl

The Premier Volleyball League (PVL) is the highest level of Volleyball played on a weekly basis in South East Queensland.

Click this logo to go to VQ's PVL page

Easts have been involved in PVL since 2004 when we started with just one team.

We are continuing to strive for good representation across the PVL competition divisions. This year, we are aiming for a Grand Prix team in addition to PVL. So this may see some changes to the teams that we enter into PVL.

2018 sees some further evolution of the PVL competition.

The junior competition will continues to consist of two divisions - under 16 and under 18. However, these competitions will continue to be truncated running only on selected weekends during the PVL season.

The Junior season will run from May to August (excluding the July school holidays. Games will be at Redlands College and The Gap SHS (girls at one venue and boys at the other, switching from week to week).

In addition, the Grand Prix competition will be running again. Some of our previous PVL players have indicated a preference for the Grand Prix competition. So this year we will be aiming to enter teams in that competition as well. More information about that competition will be added here in the near future.

How much will PVL cost for senior players?

The VQ fees for PVL teams in 2018 are:

  • Premier: $2,000
  • Division 1: $1,700
  • Division 2: $1,700

Fees for players are currently estimated as:

  • Premier Division: $455 (returning player) / $520 (new player)
  • Div 1 and Div 2: $415 (returning player) / $480 (new player)

This is based on 9 players for each team.

Please note this is just an estimate at this point in time. We have secured a training venue for the whole PVL season. However, in our past experience, unforeseen events do occur such as the need to find alternative training venues when events prevent us from using that venue.
