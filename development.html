development

eastsvolleyball have a strong commitment to junior development as this is what will keep the club sustainable

junior development squad

The Junior Development Squad is run for those players from 12 – 16 years of age. Participants in this squad work at developing and mastering their technique and base skills to reach their full potential. Skills they can take back to their school, regional and state teams. Players from this squad progress to competing in both of Brisbane’s major Volleyball Leagues with many going on to represent their Brisbane regional teams and State teams.


focus of the training squad

  • Developing solid base skills
  • Strategies for game play
  • Team building and development of teamwork skills
  • Fitness - strength, speed and agility
