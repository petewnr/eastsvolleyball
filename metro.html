metro league

Metro League is the second level of competition for volleyballers who want to play in a separate men's or women's competition.

Click this logo to go to VQ's Metro League page

It is a great opportunity for PVL players to gain some valuable match practice before their season starts, and a chance for high school teams to get regular matches and game-play experience, year-round.

eastsvolleyball currently manage the Metro League competition on behalf of QVA. The competition takes place on a Monday night at Anglican Church Grammar School (Churchie).


Key dates for 2015
Season 1 Summer 2 February to 4 May 13 weeks
Season 1 Winter 18 May to 24 August 13 weeks
Season 1 Spring 7 September to 30 November 11 weeks
