about us

Welcome to eastsvolleyball webpage. We're located in Brisbane Australia. One of our main objectives is to provide development pathways to every level of volleyball competition.

eastsvolleyball has been running as an incorporated club since 2003

Our catchment area is the inner-eastern and southern suburbs of Brisbane.

We welcome players from across all Brisbane suburbs. However, there are many excellent volleyball clubs that are based in the different areas of Brisbane. If you are looking for a club that based in a specific area, we recommend that you check out the Volleyball Queensland listing of Affiliated Clubs in South-East Queensland. Follow this link - http://www.qva.org.au/index.php?id=58

As a club, we are focused on development across the complete spectrum of volleyball. Therefore we provide training for juniors and social players through to elite players as well as the development of coaches and referees.

eastsvolleyball are currently working through a five year plan aimed at improving the sustainability of the club. A key feature of this plan is junior development. This involves creating a strong junior development program along with clear pathways for juniors both within the club and within the competitions available in the South East Queensland area.

As part of this we are actively seeking to establish connections with schools in our catchment area to ensure that younger players have an understanding of what is available for their future in volleyball and that some support is available for coaches at those schools.

Other components of this five year plan include developing coaches within the club structure and developing referees. We seek to do this by supporting people undertaking the necessary accreditation, providing opportunities to practice and develop knowledge and through providing mentoring.
