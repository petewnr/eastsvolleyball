beach squad

We love beach volleyball!

We think learning both beach and indoor skills is great for all volleyballers. They are completely different games, but each supports the other in different ways. There's a bit of a transition when moving from one to other. However, we believe the benefits are worth the effort of adjusting when moving from one season to the other.


focus of our beach squad

  • Develop sand "legs"
  • Develop fitness, strength, agility and endurance
  • Develop game strategy and awareness
  • Develop excellent ball control skills

For more information about our beach training squad, contact us via the eastsvolleyball facebook page
